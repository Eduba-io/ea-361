About the firm

Who is leaving this card.

Eduba is a veteran-owned AI research and training firm based in Palm Coast, Florida. Founder Jake Van Clief is a Marine Corps veteran (eight years, cryptographic systems, F-35 and F-18 avionics) with an MSc Future Governance from the University of Edinburgh.

The firm's published work sits adjacent to the safety-case literature Waymo already contributes to. Ethics Engine is a psychometric assessment tool for evaluating ideological and moral patterns in LLMs, available at arxiv.org/abs/2510.11742 with an open-source implementation at github.com/RinDig/AuditEngine.

The methodology paper, Interpretable Context Methodology, was submitted to ACM Transactions on Interactive Intelligent Systems. The core claim is that agent context can be organized as a layered filesystem with measurable gains in interpretability and reproducibility.
